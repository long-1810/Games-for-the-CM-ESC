DESCRIPTION: This game is like memory match, although much more extreme. Instead of matching pictures, you have to match words relating to the same topic.

    RULES

  • On the screen appear 16 words (in pairs) belonging to 8 different topics. Each topic consists of 2 words
  • Players have to connect words of the same topic until all the words are all matched.

    SCORE CALCULATION

  • Successfully match a word pair: +4 pts
  • Failed to match a word pair: +0 pts
